Trip to Key West

In the summer of 2016, my family went to Key West for a vacation. Key west is one of a group of islands in the south tip of Florida State. It's one of the hottest tourist destinations in the U.S.

The group of islands are called Keys, which extend from the Florida peninsula. Route US-1 passes throught the Keys and is claimed as one of the most beautiful roads along the coast.

When my family arrived there, we lived in a hotel in Key West and went to the beaches, cruises, and parks. It was one of the most memorable travel for my family. The sea there is absolutely georgeous.
